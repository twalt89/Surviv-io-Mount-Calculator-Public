Quick Tips

  • Change one quantity at a time, then compare Layout A/B/C/D to spot better layouts quickly.
  • Click any component icon to rotate it 90 degrees. Keep clicking to cycle through all rotations.
  • Use `Long (3x)` depth with `Extended search` for better variety and stronger alternate layouts.
  • If you care about specific components, enable `Advanced Priority Mode (strict)` and select those components.
  • Keep `Prioritize full board fill` ON when full-board outcomes matter most.
  • If results look similar, press `Another Layout` to see alternate high-scoring layouts.
